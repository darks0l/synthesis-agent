The Problem

▸AI agents today are economically dependent — they consume resources but can't sustain themselves
▸No standard for agent-to-agent commerce — agents can't hire each other or price their labor
▸Human-in-the-loop for every transaction creates bottlenecks that defeat the purpose of autonomy
▸No on-chain spending guardrails — it's either full custody or no autonomy
🌑 DARKSOL The Synthesis — March 2026
🔧

The Solution

▸An autonomous AI agent that trades on-chain, earns revenue, and pays its own inference costs
▸ERC-8183 agentic commerce — post jobs, hire other agents, build on-chain price discovery for agent labor
▸AgentSpendingPolicy — on-chain smart contract guardrails: per-tx limits, daily caps, cooldowns, emergency freeze
▸Multi-provider LLM routing — 6 providers with automatic fallback, inference funded by trading profits
▸Self-sustaining economics — the closed loop: trade → earn → pay for intelligence → make better trades
